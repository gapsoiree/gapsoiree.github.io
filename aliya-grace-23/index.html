Aliya Grace

In 2019, Aliya was accepted into the music honours program at her school which she participated in for 2 years. It was in that program that she found her love and passion for music performance. In the duration of the program, she taught herself how to play the guitar, a skill which she continues to use in her gigs. Since leaving that program in 2021, she has started gigging at a range of events and venues all around Brisbane.

Over her music journey, her repertoire has expanded from covers to some of her own original music which reflects experiences and emotions felt by both herself plus what she imagines other people to feel about certain situations. The audience can expect to hear her original songs at this year’s event.
