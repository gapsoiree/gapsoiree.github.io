Save the 2024 Date

Music Supporters Group

Save the date for the 2024 Soirée! Saturday 24th August will see more fabulous music and art return to The Gap. As usual, all funds raised from the Soirée will support the instrumental and choral music programs at The Gap State School. Show your support for live music and local artists by organising a group of family and friends to come along and enjoy a fabulous night of entertainment. Artists will be announced later in the year along with the ticket launch, so follow the Facebook or Instagram pages to be notified of the latest updates.
