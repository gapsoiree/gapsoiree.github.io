Tickets

Tickets are $20 each. The booking service charges a $0.50 fee per seat. You will be asked for the names of each attendee as part of the COVID-Safe plan.

If you have a discount code, enter the code in the Promotion Code box on the Checkout page and hit the Apply button.

Select your preferred seats below, then hit Next to go to the trybooking site to complete the purchase. In Safari, if it complains cookies not being enabled when you click Next, try selecting the seats directly on the trybooking website. And thank you for choosing to attend the Soirée!
