Coming Back in 2025

Music Supporters Group

It may seem as if the 2024 Soirée has just happened, but we are already making plans for the next. One of the first things to do is to secure a date, and now we can mark off that task as completed. Save the date for Saturday 23rd August 2025 for more showcasing of live music and art.

Live music has been taking a hit in recent years. Locally we have seen Sunlit Sounds no longer continuing, and Junk Bar is moving out of Ashgrove. We want to keep live music alive in our community, and we need your support for that. Tell your friends and family about the Soirée and bring them along. It is as simple as that.

See you at the Soirée in 2025!
