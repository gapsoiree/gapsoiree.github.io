Beetle Miyela

Music Supporters Group

Beetle Miyela is a sound and performance artist, her work explores a personal relationship with courageous vulnerability. Dedicated to creating safe spaces to journey people back to their centre, she uses art as a medium to bridge the philosophy, the imaginary and the extraordinary. Beetle draws source from the duality of oneness, the overlap and meeting places of two, becoming one.
