June 23, 2021

Official Launch

We are pleased to announce the full line-up for the Soirée on 11 September 2021.

We have a great range of artists ready to delight with different styles of music.

  • Slade Gibson and Melissa Gill
  • Tessitura Acapella
  • MINT
  • Duo Key
  • Brisbane Folk Quartet
  • The Vocalise Duo

More information about the artists will be revealed in the coming weeks on this website and on our socials, so make sure to follow us on Facebook or Twitter to keep up to date with all of the latest news.

Tickets will go on sale on July 8. It will be allocated seating, so get in quick to choose your preferred seats. Along with requiring attendees to scan in using the Check In Qld app, this also helps to maintain compliance with current COVID restrictions defined by QLD Health. These measures will help us all to stay safe and be able to enjoy more of these live music events featuring local artists from our beautiful city.

We are looking forward to seeing you at the Soirée in September!
