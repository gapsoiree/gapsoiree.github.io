About Us

The Soirée, a night of fabulous music, is organised and run by the Music Supporters Group, a sub-committee of The Gap State School P&C Association. Funds raised by the event go towards supporting the instrumental and choral programs at The Gap State School. This support includes funding the purchase and service of instruments, purchase of sheet music and equipment, supply and maintenance of music uniforms, and assistance with funding of teacher and administration time above that provided by the Queensland Department of Education.

The Gap State School maintains a highly regarded instrumental and choral program, and members of the Music Supporters Group help to keep the music program at a very high standard by letting the teachers do what they do best; teach. In addition to funding support, we also help to run the end-of-semester instrumental and choral concerts and do other fund-raising activities throughout the year.

We can be contacted via email at msg@tgsspc.org.au.
