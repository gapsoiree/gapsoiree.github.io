Duo Key

Glyn Hickling is a trumpet and flugelhorn player who has played in a variety of settings, including classical, jazz, reggae, funk, and ska and was a tragic 90's Brisbane punk. Currently he is a member of a working Ska and Soul band and also participates in the local Brass Band Association playing in concerts and competitions.

Kerry Beaumont is a local piano teacher and accompanist. She’s been playing for schools in The Gap since 2009. She has a keen interest in healthy piano technique and has been studying the Taubman Technique with Therese Milanovic since 2013. She also dabbles in jazz piano, and is currently playing with TBQ Quintet.

They will be playing pieces from a repertoire of Romantic and 20th Century era music, with maybe a sneaky jazz number thrown in.
