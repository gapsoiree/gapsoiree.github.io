March 29, 2022

2022 Tickets Now Available

Tickets are now available for the 2022 Soirée, which is guaranteed to be another night of fabulous music! Head over to the Tickets page to secure your seats at the new lower price of $20. Also remember to like and follow the Facebook page to see artist announcements as they are released.

With a couple of groups returning from the successful 2021 Soirée along with a bunch of new musicians, the night will have something for everyone with a fresh variety of musical styles. Artist announcements will happen over the coming weeks. Also returning is the art gallery, the beer and wine, the food, and of course the fantastic atmosphere.

So come along, bring your family, friends and neighbours, and join other members of the community to enjoy a night of fabulous music and great company. Help to support local musicians and also the instrumental and choral programs at The Gap State School.

Grab your tickets, and we'll see you at the Soirée!
