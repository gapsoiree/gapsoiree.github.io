The Soirée, a night of fabulous music, is a fund-raiser for the instrumental and choral music programs at The Gap State School.

Meet the bands

Rooted in the rich traditions of Irish, Scottish, and Australian music, Amaidí are ready to shake the walls with absolutely pumping tunes.

Blue Poles is a contemporary classical trio that takes inspiration from the explosive yet intricate quality of Jackson Pollock’s painting of the same name.

Wolf’s Tales is a contemporary folk rock band that invite their audiences to tap their feet, listen to heart-warming stories, laugh, smile or sing along.

With diverse influences spanning 60s surf rock, 70s folk and 00s indie punk, Vacant Gaze and their shimmering, jangly guitars and ethereal vocals offer something for everyone.

 

FAQ

When is the next Soirée?

The next Soirée is on Saturday 23rd August 2025. Make sure you save the date!

Where is it held?

It is held at The Gap State School in the MPB (Multi-Purpose Building), accessed from Settlement Rd.

What time does it start?

Doors open from 5pm with pre-show entertainment, wine and food. The show starts at 6:30pm, with an interval around 7:40pm. It is expected to finish around 9:30pm.

When will tickets be available?

Tickets are available now at Humanitix!

Who is performing this year?

The acts this year include the return of Amaidí, who last performed at 2023, and the introduction of Blue Poles, Wolf's Tales and Vacant Gaze.

Where does the artwork come from?

The artworks are provided by members of The Gap Creative Inc. The artworks are available to purchase, so you can own your piece of locally created artwork for display in your home or business.

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

Yes, it is easily accessible with a wheelchair. There are no steps and the floor is flat.
