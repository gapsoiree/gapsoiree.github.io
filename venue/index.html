Venue

The venue is the Multi-Purpose Building at The Gap State School, cnr Waterworks Rd & Settlement Rd, The Gap in the western suburbs of Brisbane. The main entrance to the MPB is on Settlement Rd, opposite Bromar St. Parking will be available at this entrance or along Settlement Rd. Alternative parking and entrance can be found along Allambic St behind the school.
