September 1, 2021

It's Getting Exciting

With just over a week to go until the Soirée, a night of fabulous music, is upon us, the excitement is building. The musicians are rehearsing their sets, the decor is being arranged, the bar is being stocked, and tickets are being sold. There are still good tickets available, so get in soon to lock in yours for family and friends.

Start the night off with a drink from the bar and view the art display from students at The Gap State High School, with music provided by selected senior instrumental music students from The Gap State School.

During the interval, the String Quartet from The Gap State High School will perform in the foyer near the art display while you enjoy another drink from the bar.

The music will delight, with cello and piano by The Vocalise Duo, Australian, Scandinavian and UK folk music by Brisbane Folk Quartet, and a mix of classical and jazz trumpet and flugelhorn with piano by Duo Key. The guitar and vocals of Slade Gibson and Melissa Gill will draw you in, the vocal harmony of Tessitura Acapella will carry you along to the eclectic and diverse original repertoire of MINT.

So get in quick to secure your tickets, and we will see you at the Soirée!
