September 10, 2023

Get Your Tickets Now

It is now only one more week until the Soirée for 2023 brings another round of fabulous music. Grab your tickets now for this wonderful event! Look at this wonderful line-up.

Music from Aliya Grace, The Aristocats, Rebecca Wright & Donald McKay, Amaidí, Robert Bloodwood, and Monique Clare. Pre-show and interval music provided by the Rock Band and Cello Choir from The Gap State High School. Art exhibition by the creatives at The Gap Creative with the pieces available for purchase.  Beer, wine and food available to keep those cravings satisfied. A raffle with prizes donated by Cr Steven Toomey and Jonty Bush MP. Some artist merchandise available.

Doors open at 5pm. Pre-show entertainment at 5:45pm. Show starts at 6:30pm. Make sure you arrive early enough to take in the art exhibition and pre-show entertainment.

There is something for everyone here, and you may even find something new that you hadn't seen before. So head on over to the Tickets page and we will...

See you at the Soirée!
