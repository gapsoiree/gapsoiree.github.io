Aliya Grace

Aliya's music journey started back in 2014 at The Gap State School where she took part in the music program learning how to read and play music as well as partake in the school choir, starting her love of music and performing. When she reached high school, her music took a new turn when she was accepted into the music honours class, taking her understanding to the next level. In that class she was faced with music analysis, theory, composition, and performance.

After her first performance, she taught herself how to play the guitar as well as a bit of piano and started covering a range of different music. From that point, she set up her own Instagram page and started posting covers. She began writing a bit of her own music which she now includes in her repertoire. She started off by performing a few gigs for her school which has now expanded out to cafés, charities, wine tastings and a range of other events.
