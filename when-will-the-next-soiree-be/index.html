The Next Soirée?

Music Supporters Group

After a bit of downtime to recover from the Soirée of 2021, we are now back with planning for the 2022 event. When will that be, we hear you ask.

We have booked in Saturday 4th June, 2022 for the next Soirée. That time in the third quarter of the year was quite busy for many people, and looking ahead it appears it will be similar again. So we made the decision to move it a bit earlier in the year. It will be bigger and better, with another collection of local musicians and groups to entertain. There will be beer and wine. There will be more glorious food. There will be another beautiful art gallery. And of course there will be you, our wonderful patrons to enjoy the night. There will be more announcements in early 2022, so keep in touch for all the updates.

If you have a business and would be interested in sponsoring the Soirée in 2022, please contact us. There are many costs to run an event such as this, and sponsorship helps us to provide the tickets at a reasonable price.

See you at the next Soirée!
