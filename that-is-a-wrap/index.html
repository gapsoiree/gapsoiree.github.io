September 13, 2021

That is a Wrap!

And that is a wrap for another night of fabulous music at the Soirée. It was a long day, setting up, the event, then cleaning up, my body was aching and sore, but it was a fantastic night and I loved every minute of it.
 
Thank you to all of the volunteers who helped with the night. You made this night run smoothly and it was a pleasure to watch everything happen.
 
Thank you to the sponsors. Concept Carpentry and Ray White The Gap. Cr Steven Toomey and the Lord Mayor's Community Fund. Jonty Bush - Member for Cooper and all of the businesses that donated prizes for the raffles.
 
Thank you to all of the performers who played for us. It was indeed some fabulous music, and we loved listening to it. Thank you for sharing it with us, including the senior instrumental students from The Gap State School and the String Quartet from The Gap State High School, the school that also provided the wonderful visual art displays.
 
And thank you to everyone who attended the night. It is you that make this night possible. Seeing everyone enjoying a great night out is what drives us to do this.
See you at the next Soirée!
