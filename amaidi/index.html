Amaidí

Are you ready to dance? Amaidí is here to shake the walls with absolutely pumping tunes! Rooted in the rich traditions of Irish, Scottish, and Australian music, this four-piece band pushes the boundaries of neo-trad with electrifying energy, driving rhythms, and bold contemporary twists.

From blistering reels and powerhouse songs to soul-stirring ballads that stop time in its tracks, Amaidí plays music that is pure, heart-racing, and goosebump-inducing music. Whether it’s a rip-roaring trad set or a hauntingly beautiful song, their sound is fierce, fresh, and impossible to resist.
